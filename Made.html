Made-To-Order Process

At Joy in A Box, we create personalized décor items tailored to your style and needs.

  1. Choose a Product: Select the type of item you want customized.
  2. Send Your Design: Share your ideas, colors, or inspiration with us.
  3. Confirmation: We review your request and confirm details.
  4. Production: Your item is carefully created with attention to detail.
  5. Delivery: Your custom product is packaged and delivered to you.
